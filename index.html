ETHOS

Oranges is founded on three principles:

Art should be accessible to as many people as possible;

artists must be fairly compensated for their labor;

&

art has the capacity, both socially & economically, to effect positive change in our communities & world.

Process

ORANGES operates in 4 phases:

1

Development

Artists are invited to develop a release & mutually select a cause or nonprofit to support.

2

Production

Works are made by the artists’ own hands or in collaboration with expertprinters or fabricators.

3

Distribution of works

Works are offered for sale online & made available to the widest possible audience.

4

Distribution of funds

Profits are shared, supporting the ongoing work of artists, Oranges & causes or nonprofits.

Background

Oranges was initiated by artist MATTHEW BOOTH,

whose previously co-organized artist edition projects, Pictures for Elmhurst & States of Change, have raised more than $3 million for public health & social justice causes.
